About Me

I grew up on a small farm in Iowa for most of my life where I helped my dad out with various chores. This inspired a life-long passion for taking care of animals and a love for the outdoors. Even though I love the quiet beauty of Iowa, my husband and I decided to make a move to Orlando Florida about six months ago.

So far, my favorite thing about living in Florida is the fact that the Atlantic Ocean or the Gulf are only an hour and a half drive away. I aspire to learn how to surf sometime this year, once I can find the time! I try to keep a healthy work/life balance and while I enjoy learning and can be consumed by a new topic, I try to take a break and relax with a good thriller or action movie. I also like to spend my down time reading books that focus on worlds of fantasy and science fiction.
